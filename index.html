About Me

My curiosity in computer science rose during lockdown, when the world was brought to a halt, and technology acted as a means to allow people to carry on with school, work, and their lives. I’ve always found the ability to stay connected with communities from the comfort of homes to be captivating and was intrigued with the inner workings of how it all functioned. I thoroughly enjoy being creative, and I find it to align with the inherent flexibility in technology’s uses and construction, solidifying my desire to pursue a career in this field.
